# Magellanicclouds
I collect a range of resources here on making obscure or less considered topics more accessible.

I have three other websites that may be of interested.
The first is a blog where I review a range of board games and explain the process I have taken to make them accessible for myself available at https://blindboardgaming.magellanicclouds.net
The second is a web application that allows people to look at maps and geospatial datasets accessibly https://accessible-gis.magellanicclouds.net/
A working demonstration of this web app in action with a map of country boarders is viewable here https://accessible-gis.magellanicclouds.net/?vector=countries_simplified.geojson
The final one is a simple dnd character sheet manager that is 100% screen reader accessible https://dnd.magellanicclouds.net
