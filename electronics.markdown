# Electronics
Many many electronics tutorials rely heavily on the use of videos and pictures to explain how to construct things or how to connect components together.

Here I intend to consolidate a range of useful resources that are either intended for use by blind electronics hobbiests or that are usable without sight.

I will cover a broard range of topics from constructing home made radio telescopes to manufacturing semiconductors from scratch to simply detailing what pins do what on common micro controller boards.

Where resources do not exist or may be lacking I intend to fill them in with my own explainations and tutorials.

I will primarily focus on topics that I am currently interested in however I hope this may grow to become a useful resource for other blind hobbiests wanting to explore similar projects.
